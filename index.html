Who am I?

Born in Ohio, I spent my early childhood in Birmingham, AL, before finishing middle school in Cleveland, OH. I graduated from Shaker Heights High School in 2018. After studying at The Ohio State University, I graduated Cum Laude in 2022 with a B.S. in Electrical and Computer Engineering and a minor in Robotics and Autonomous Systems. I've returned to Cleveland to work in architectural engineering consulting, with a portfolio that includes theaters, laboratories, hospitals and libraries.

Growing up, I fenced competitively and studied the flute. I enjoy reading; many of my journal posts result from my reflections on books and articles that I've read. I contributed to the IT and Outreach programs of my university's MSA and I'm now active with my masjid community.
