Who am I?

I'm an engineer based in Cleveland, OH. I work for an architectural engineering consulting firm, designing electrical systems for hospitals, theaters, laboratories and universities. I moved to Cleveland in 2013 after spending my early childhood in Birmingham, AL, and left only for a couple years to study at Ohio State. You'll notice most of the site is devoted to journal entries. Writing forces me to engage with experiences and media in an active, structured and reflective way. I hope it makes for interesting reading!
